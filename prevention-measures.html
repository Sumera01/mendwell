Prevention Measures

Explore essential preventive steps to protect your health and well-being.

Hand Hygiene

Regular handwashing with soap and water for at least 20 seconds can help prevent the spread of infections.

Vaccination

Vaccines play a critical role in building immunity against various diseases, ensuring community health.

Healthy Diet

A balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and whole grains boosts the immune system and prevents chronic diseases.

Physical Exercise

Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight, strengthens muscles, and reduces the risk of many diseases.
